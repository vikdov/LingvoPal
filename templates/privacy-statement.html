II. Privacy Policy

A. Introduction

The language learning support platform attaches great importance to the protection of the privacy of its users and undertakes to handle their personal data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, to whom it may be disclosed, and what rights users have in connection with their personal data. This policy applies to all users of the Platform, both registered and unregistered.

B. What Information We Collect

When registering an account on the Platform, the user may be asked to provide personal data such as name, email address, login and password, preferred learning language, and language proficiency level. In addition, when using the Google login option, the Platform may gain access to some information from the user's Google account, in accordance with the user's privacy settings on that account. The Platform also automatically collects certain information regarding the use of the Platform, such as the device's IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on the Platform, and user activity within the Platform. While using the Platform, data regarding the user's learning progress, language preferences, flashcard game results, interactions with the AI assistant, and use of the contextual learning mode are also collected. When using the browser extension, the Platform may collect information about the websites viewed by the user and analyze texts to generate exercises. If the user uses the audio exercise function, the Platform may collect the user's voice recordings.

C. How We Use Your Information

The collected personal data is used to provide the Platform's services, personalize the learning process, monitor user progress, improve the Platform's functionality, and communicate with users. Data regarding user progress and their language preferences may be used to generate personalized learning recommendations. Information about user activity on the Platform and data from the browser extension may be analyzed to improve the contextual learning mode and provide more relevant exercises. Audio recordings may be used to assess the user's pronunciation and to improve the speech recognition function of the AI assistant. The Platform may also use anonymous and aggregated data for statistical and research purposes, to analyze trends and optimize the Platform.

D. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

Users' personal data may be disclosed to third parties only in specific situations. The Platform may use the services of external providers to provide certain services, such as hosting, payment processing, data analysis, or sending email notifications. In such cases, users' personal data may be disclosed to these providers to the extent necessary for them to provide services on behalf of the Platform. In the case of logging in via a Google account, the Platform may exchange certain information with Google to authenticate the user. Content generated by users, such as created flashcard sets, may be shared with other users of the Platform, in accordance with the user's privacy settings. The Platform may also disclose users' personal data to law enforcement agencies or other public authorities if required by applicable law.

E. Data Security

The Platform makes every effort to protect users' personal data against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction. For this purpose, appropriate technical and organizational measures are used, such as data encryption, access control, and regular security audits. Users are also responsible for the security of their account, including maintaining the confidentiality of their login and password.

F. Data Retention

Users' personal data will be stored for the period necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After this period, users' personal data will be permanently deleted or anonymized. Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data from the Platform, in accordance with the principles described in section G.

G. Your Rights

Users have the right to access their personal data stored by the Platform, to correct, update, or delete it. In addition, users have the right to restrict the processing of their data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, the user should contact the Platform using the contact details provided in section P.

H. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Platform uses cookies and other tracking technologies to improve the functionality of the Platform, personalize content, and analyze user traffic. Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device that help the Platform remember the user's preferences and track their activity. Users have the ability to manage cookie settings in their browser.

I. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not directed at children under the age of [insert appropriate age]. The Platform does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a parent or legal guardian becomes aware that their child has provided the Platform with personal data without their consent, they should contact the Platform, and this data will be deleted.

J. Personalized Recommendations

The Platform uses data about user activity, their learning progress, and language preferences to provide personalized learning recommendations. The user has the option to disable personalized recommendations in their account settings.

K. Privacy of the Browser Extension

The browser extension may collect data about the websites viewed by the user, including URLs and text content, in order to provide services such as translating words and generating exercises. This data is used solely for the purpose of providing these services and is not shared with third parties without the user's consent. The user can disable the extension or change its settings at any time. The extension asks for specific permissions during installation, such as access to data on websites. The user should familiarize themselves with these permissions before installing the extension.

L. Privacy of Audio Data

When using the audio exercise function or voice interaction with the AI assistant, the Platform may collect the user's voice recordings. This data is used to assess pronunciation, provide personalized guidance, and improve speech recognition functions. The user will be asked for their consent to record and use their voice. Audio recordings are stored for a limited time and are protected against unauthorized access.

M. Learning Analytics Data

The Platform may collect and analyze data regarding how users use the Platform, including learning progress, most common mistakes, and average learning time. This data is used to improve the Platform, personalize the learning process, and provide more effective educational materials. Analytical data is usually anonymized and aggregated.

N. International Data Transfer (If Applicable)

Users' personal data may be stored and processed on servers located in various countries, including outside the European Economic Area. In the event of data transfer outside the EEA, the Platform ensures that this data is protected in accordance with applicable law, including through the use of standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

he Platform reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be informed of any changes by posting the updated version of the Privacy Policy on the Platform or by sending an email notification. Changes take effect on the date of their publication, unless otherwise specified.
